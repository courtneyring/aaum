Board Leadership

The club is led by a Board of Directors who are elected from the general club membership and Executive Committee Officers. There are also several committees made up of club members just like you that help plan the Club's activities. Elections are held in the first quarter of each year for a three year term of 1/3 of the Directors. Additionally, the club has created new slots on the board for younger members, namely those who have graduated within six years of election to the Board, to encourage more recent graduates into the club operations. The board works on behalf of the members of the Club.

If you want to know more or to attend meetings, please email the club at umclub-nyc@umich.edu.
