On behalf of the Alumni Association of the University of Michigan, we’d like to invite you to join the U of M Club of New York, your connection back to all things Michigan!

The U of M Club of New York is part of a network of regional clubs within the U.S. that are affiliated with the Alumni Association. Alumni who are paid members of the Alumni Association are automatically a member of the U of M Club of New York, with no additional dues to pay.

The dues paid by Alumni Association members help provide the U of M Club of New York with the financial resources, communication tools and support that local club volunteer alumni need to host club events.
