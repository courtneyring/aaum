Information
  • Must be a member of the alumni association. If you are not, you can join here.
  • Tickets for all home games will be in the south end zone sections of the stadium
  • Ticket prices are anticipated to be in the range of $55-$200 each, depending upon the game
  • Homecoming game ticket prices will also include the pre-game AA sponsored tailgate and festivities (an additional $30-60)
  • Deadline to order is Sunday March 10
  • There are no maximum limits to ordering tickets, we cannot make any guarantees of the club's allotment
  • If requests cannot be fully met, then tickets will be rationed, or a lottery system will be employed
  • Group orders should be made and paid for by a single individual to ensure everyone in the group are seated together
  • If payment and delivery is to be made by a person other than the person ordering the tickets, include their name in your email
